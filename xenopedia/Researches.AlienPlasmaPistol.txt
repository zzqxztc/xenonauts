<desc>The Plasma Pistol is an alien sidearm that is approximately eight inches long and roughly a kilogram in weight. Though it bears some resemblance to a human firearm, it does not fire a bullet but rather a searing bolt of superheated plasma.&#10;&#10;It is a powerful weapon, easily capable of killing an unarmoured man in a single shot. However, our tests suggest the compact size of the weapon renders it fairly inefficient: the plasma bolt loses cohesion and starts to dissipate almost immediately. It is thus most dangerous at short ranges, where a single shot has a good chance of killing one of our soldiers outright. As that range increases, the projectile steadily becomes less accurate and damaging. The lesson here should be clear: fight units equipped with this weapon from as far away as possible. &#10;&#10;Though it would make a fine addition to our arsenal, this weapon has clearly been designed for alien troops. Anatomical differences between our species make it difficult for a human to wield effectively, so any soldier attempting to use this weapon on the battlefield will suffer severe accuracy penalties when doing so. Sadly, we are also nowhere near understanding or replicating the technology contained within the weapon (recovering more advanced alien weapons may help in that regard). All is not lost, however - running more tests on this weapon should allow us to develop battlefield infantry armour better suited to protecting our men from extraterrestrial energy weapons.</desc>
<cdesc>外星等离子手枪是一种外星副手武器，长约8英寸，重约1公斤。虽然和人类武器在外观上相似，但等离子手枪发射的并不是子弹，而是一团灼热的等离子体。&#10;&#10;等离子手枪威力十足，一发等离子子弹就足以杀死一个没有护甲保护的人类。然而，我们的实验表明，这件武器有限的体积降低了其威力：等离子子弹几乎在发射的同时就失去了内聚力，并开始解体。因此，等离子手枪仅在近距离射击的情况下才最危险，一发等离子子弹就很可能彻底杀死我们的一名士兵。随着射击距离的增长，等离子子弹的精确性和威力都逐渐降低。我们能得出一个结论：尽可能在远距离和装备有这种武器的敌人交战。&#10;&#10;虽然能够将等离子手枪扩充进我们的兵工厂，但它显然是为外星部队设计的。解剖学上的区别，使得人类很难使用这种武器。任何想在战场上使用这种武器的士兵，射击准确率都会大大降低。更加不幸的是，我们目前还无法理解或是复制这件武器中的技术（通过夺取和研究更高级的外星武器，可能会对这一点有所帮助）。但还有一线希望 - 通过对这件武器进行更多的实验，我们可以开发出新型的战斗护甲，来帮助我们的士兵更好地抵抗外星能量武器。</cdesc>
